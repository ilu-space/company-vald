Built with Responsibility

Sustainability isn't
a footnote here.

We built responsibility into the process from the first sketch — not as a marketing layer applied after the fact.

🌿

Recycled & Responsible Fabrics

We prioritise recycled synthetics and responsibly sourced natural blends across every collection. No greenwashing — the material source is listed on every product page.

📦

Plastic-Free Packaging

Every VALD order ships in compostable or recycled paper packaging. No poly bags, no tissue paper waste, no unnecessary inserts.

🤝

Transparent Manufacturing

We name our manufacturing partners and share the standards they're held to. Every product page links to a transparency report — because accountability shouldn't be optional.

FAQ

Honest answers.

The questions we'd be asking if we were you.

We're preparing Collection I — FJORD — for its debut drop. Join the VALD Circle waitlist below to get first access before the public launch, along with early pricing and the ability to reserve your size.
Every piece in the VALD range comes with a detailed fit guide using real-body measurement references — not just generic S/M/L descriptions. If your first order isn't the right fit, our exchange process is simple, fast, and free of charge. We'd rather you keep the gear than send it back.
It means we validate our fabric choices and construction in Finnish conditions — cold, wet, and variable. If a material performs across temperature extremes and year-round training in the Nordic climate, it'll handle wherever you are. It's our own bar, not a certification — but it's a high one.
We'll link every claim to its source. Recycled material percentages, manufacturing partners, packaging specs — all visible on each product page. We're not perfect, and we say so. But everything we claim will be verifiable from launch day. No vague language like "eco-conscious" without substance behind it.
Most premium sportswear still trades on logo recognition and seasonal volume. VALD is a smaller, more deliberate operation — intentional drops, no overproduction, no loud branding. We're a D2C brand which means no retail markups and a direct relationship with the people buying. Fewer pieces, made better, sold honestly.
VALD Circle is our loyalty and early-access programme. Circle members get first look at new drops before they go public, access to exclusive colorways that don't appear in the general store, and member pricing on selected pieces. It doesn't cost anything to join — it's our way of rewarding people who believe in what we're building early.
Our launch focus is the Nordic and European market, with international shipping available to most destinations. Full shipping details, carrier options, and estimated delivery windows will be published at launch. Join the waitlist and we'll notify you with all the specifics once we're live.
