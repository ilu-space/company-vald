Our Design Philosophy

Less, but better.

"Great sportswear shouldn't force you to choose between looking sharp and performing at your best."

VALD was created for men who've grown tired of the compromise — either technical gear that looks purely functional, or stylish clothes that fall apart under real physical demands.

We apply Scandinavian design discipline to every silhouette, every seam, every stitch. The result is a wardrobe that transitions effortlessly from a strength session to a coffee meeting — without changing a thing.

Sustainability is foundational, not optional
Nordic Minimalism

Clean silhouettes, restrained palettes, and purposeful details — no excess noise, only intentional design.

Technical Performance

Every fabric is selected for moisture control, stretch, breathability, and durability under high-intensity use.

Versatile Utility

Designed for the full arc of a modern man's day — gym, street, café — without looking like gym gear in a café.

Responsible Materials

Recycled fibres, low-impact dyes, and packaging that doesn't outlast the planet. Built to wear longer, toss less.

Fabric Innovation

What VALD is made of.

We start with the material and work outward. Every fabric we use earns its place with measurable performance credentials.

Recycled Polyester

Sourced from post-consumer plastics. High-tenacity, moisture-wicking, and engineered to retain shape through hundreds of washes.

Merino-Tech Blend

Natural temperature regulation and odour resistance of Merino wool, reinforced with technical fibres for endurance and stretch performance.

Supplex Nylon

Ultra-soft, cotton-like hand feel combined with nylon's superior abrasion resistance, packability, and colour retention.

Organic Cotton Blend

For our athleisure pieces — breathable, soft, and grown without synthetic pesticides. Blended with elastane for everyday ease of movement.

Our Commitments

We hold ourselves to a higher standard than the industry default.

✓

No greenwashing. We only make sustainability claims we can substantiate with specific material certifications or measurable process improvements.

✓

Anti-fast fashion model. We release curated drops, not constant newness. Fewer SKUs, deeper quality, longer product lives.

✓

Supply chain transparency. We'll publish where and how our garments are made — because you deserve to know what you're wearing.

✓

Care & repair guidance. Every product ships with guidance on extending its lifespan — wash cold, air dry, wear longer.

✓

Ongoing improvement. We're not perfect yet. We commit to publishing our sustainability progress annually and setting harder targets each cycle.

A Note from the Founder

"I built VALD because I couldn't find what I wanted to wear — and I was tired of settling."

Every time I walked into a sportswear store, I had to choose: do I want something that performs, or something I'm actually proud to wear? The technical stuff looked clinical. The lifestyle stuff fell apart after six months. The gap between those two options is exactly where VALD lives.

I grew up in Finland, where design is taken seriously as a discipline — not a decoration. Where simplicity is considered a form of respect for the person using the thing. I wanted to apply that thinking to sportswear. Not just make something that looks Nordic, but build something that thinks Nordic: functional, considered, durable, honest.

This is still early. We're building in public, and we'd rather be honest with you about that than pretend we've been around for a decade. If you believe in the same things we do — quality over quantity, form and function as inseparable, sustainability as a real commitment — we'd love to have you with us from the start.

— The Founder, VALD
Helsinki, Finland
